Disclaimer & Notes

This calculator uses simplified assumptions for demonstration. Actual tax results depend on your unique situation, potential state taxes, changing laws, and personal deductions. Consult a tax professional for a definitive strategy.

Key Points:
- Sole Prop & Partnership: We apply official self-employment tax logic (92.35% net earnings, SS wage base limit, 2.9% Medicare).
- S Corp: We assume only the W-2 salary is subject to payroll taxes. The remaining profit is taken as distributions (no SS/Medicare). We add “extra fees” if you input them.
- C Corp: We assume 21% federal corporate tax on leftover profit after owner salary, plus a 15% dividend tax on that leftover (100% distributed). We also apply payroll taxes on the salary portion.

State-level complexities (like CA franchise fees, multi-layered nexus, or pass-through entity taxes) are beyond the scope of this simplified calculator.

We hope this comparison helps you see broad differences. For in-depth analysis, reach out to CPA On Fire for professional assistance.
