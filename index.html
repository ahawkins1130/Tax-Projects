About Our Calculator

Our S Corp Calculator is designed to provide a rough estimate of potential tax savings when operating your business as an S Corporation versus a Sole Proprietorship/LLC (disregarded entity). While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, tax situations vary widely, and this tool should be considered a starting point for discussions with a qualified professional.

We continuously update our assumptions to reflect current tax rates and thresholds, but be aware that federal and state laws change frequently. Always consult with a CPA or tax advisor to interpret these results and customize them to your situation.
