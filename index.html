Disclaimers & Notes

Sole Proprietorship & Partnership:
- We apply official self-employment tax logic (92.35% factor, 12.4% SS up to wage base, 2.9% Medicare).
- We then apply your chosen personal rate to the entire net income.

S Corporation:
- Only the salary portion is subject to payroll tax (SS + Medicare).
- The entire net income (including distribution) is subject to personal tax at the chosen rate.

State taxes, multi-owner complexities, or specific deductions may alter real-world outcomes. This calculator is intended for broad illustration. Consult CPA On Fire for professional advice.
