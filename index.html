About Our S Corp Calculator

At CPA On Fire, we specialize in helping businesses optimize their tax strategies. This calculator provides an approximation of potential tax savings if you elect to file as an S Corporation rather than remain a sole proprietor or single-member LLC. Using current tax assumptions, we estimate self-employment taxes, payroll taxes, and other costs.

Please note that every business is unique and state/federal laws change frequently. Consult a qualified CPA to interpret these results in light of your individual circumstances.
